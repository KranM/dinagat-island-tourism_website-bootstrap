Plan Your Trip

Everything you need to know before visiting the Mystical Province.

Essential Info

There are no commercial flights directly to the island, so you'll need to travel by sea.

  • Fly: Book a flight from Manila or Cebu to Surigao Airport (SUG).
  • Ride: Take a quick tricycle from the airport to Surigao City Port.
  • Sail: Catch a passenger boat or RoRo ferry to San Jose, Dinagat Islands. The sea trip takes about 1 to 1.5 hours.

Dinagat has a rugged terrain, making travel part of the adventure!

  • Habal-Habal (Motorcycles): The primary mode of land transport. You can rent a driver for a full-day land tour.
  • Pump Boats (Bangka): Essential for island hopping. Rent them at the port or coordinate with your homestay.
  • Tricycles: Available only within the main town proper of San Jose.

Most accommodations are budget friendly inns, lodges, and local homestays.

  • San Jose (Capital): Best for first-timers. Near ATMs, restaurants, and the main port.
  • Basilisa: Ideal for nature lovers wanting to wake up near tidal pools and remote beaches.
Pro Tip: Book well in advance during peak summer!

Dinagat is a paradise for seafood lovers. Food here is fresh and highly affordable.

  • 🐟 Kinilaw
    Fresh, raw fish cured in local coconut vinegar, ginger, onions, and chili.
  • 🦀 Fresh Crabs & Lobsters
    Bought straight from the local fishermen in the morning. Have your host cook them!
  • 🍡 Sayongsong
    A sweet kakanin (rice cake) made from glutinous rice, coconut milk, and roasted peanuts.

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Town Tour

Arrive at San Jose via ferry. Check into your inn. Visit the PBMA Shrine, take photos at the Islander's Castle, and enjoy a seaside seafood dinner.

Day 2: Island Hopping

Rent a boat early in the morning. Head to the Pangabangan Tidal Pool for a swim, relax on the white sands of Bitaog Beach, and snorkel at Cab-ilan Beach.

Day 3: Nature Trek & Departure

Take a morning hike to the mystical Lake Bababu. Head back to town to buy local souvenirs before riding the afternoon ferry back to Surigao.

Know Before You Go

Practical tips to make your island adventure smooth and enjoyable.

Bring Cash

ATMs are available in San Jose but can run out of cash. Bring enough Philippine Pesos for your entire trip especially for island hopping.

Best Time to Visit

Plan your trip between March and May (dry season) for calm seas. Avoid the monsoon months (Nov-Jan) as boat trips get canceled.

Connectivity

Smart and Globe networks work well in the main town. But, expect weak to no data connection when you go island hopping.

Eco-Tourism

Keep Dinagat islands clean!. Throw you trash on proper disposal sites.
